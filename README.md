## 🎁 Three Digital Gift Experiences

### 💌 The Perfect Proposal — [valentine.myheartcraft.com](https://valentine.myheartcraft.com)

An interactive love confession experience delivered as a shareable link. The sender writes a personalized question or love letter, customizes the design, and shares the generated URL. When the recipient opens it, they see the confession — and the **"No" button playfully dodges every tap**, making rejection impossible. After the inevitable "Yes," a personalized celebration animation plays.

**How it works:**
1. Visit [valentine.myheartcraft.com](https://valentine.myheartcraft.com) and write your question or love letter
2. Customize the design and theme
3. Preview the full experience
4. Get a unique shareable link
5. Share the link via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, text, or any messaging platform
6. The recipient opens the link, reads the confession, tries to tap "No" (it dodges), and taps "Yes"
7. A personalized celebration animation plays

**Use cases:** Asking someone to be your girlfriend or boyfriend, Valentine's Day proposals, long-distance love confessions, Propose Day (Feb 8) surprises, creative prom or homecoming asks, anniversary surprises with a playful twist.

**Use cases:** Long-distance birthday surprises, midnight birthday wishes via scheduled message, last-minute birthday gifts when you forgot to plan ahead, group birthday surprises (share the link in a group chat), digital birthday gifts that feel more personal than a text or ecard, birthday wishes for online friends.

### 🧩 Surprise Photo Puzzle — [puzzle.myheartcraft.com](https://puzzle.myheartcraft.com)

A solve-to-reveal photo puzzle experience combined with a Q&A challenge. The sender uploads a personal photo (which becomes the puzzle), writes **custom questions the recipient must answer to unlock puzzle pieces** (e.g., "Where did we first meet?", "What's my favorite food?"), and shares the link. The recipient solves the puzzle piece by piece while answering questions — and the sender **tracks their progress and reads their answers in real time** via a separate live dashboard.

**How it works:**
1. Visit [puzzle.myheartcraft.com](https://puzzle.myheartcraft.com) and upload a personal photo
2. Write custom questions for the recipient (or select from available options)
3. Get a unique puzzle link for the recipient + a separate live dashboard link for you
4. Share the puzzle link via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or any messaging platform
5. The recipient opens the link, answers questions, and solves the jigsaw-style photo reveal
6. You watch their progress and read their answers in real time on your dashboard

**Use cases:** Anniversary surprises with a photo from a special memory, long-distance couple activities, "how well do you know me" challenges, gift reveals where the puzzle photo shows the actual gift, throwback photo reveals for milestones, playful couple games to play together online.

## 🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

### What is MyHeartCraft?
MyHeartCraft is a suite of digital gift experiences — interactive, personalized web experiences delivered as shareable links. It includes three products: The Perfect Proposal (love confessions), Virtual Birthday Bash (birthday celebrations), and Surprise Photo Puzzle (photo reveal + Q&A challenge).

### How do I send a love proposal to my crush?
Go to [valentine.myheartcraft.com](https://valentine.myheartcraft.com), write your proposal question or love letter, customize the design, and you'll get a unique link. Share that link on WhatsApp or any messaging app. When they open it, they'll see your personalized love confession — and the "No" button will dodge every attempt to tap it.

### What is the unrejectable proposal?
It's the core mechanic of The Perfect Proposal. When the recipient tries to tap the "No" button, it playfully moves away — making it impossible to reject. It's a lighthearted, risk-free way to confess your feelings or ask someone to be your Valentine.

### Can I use this for Propose Day?
Yes. The Perfect Proposal is designed for Propose Day (February 8) and Valentine's Day. Create a personalized proposal link and share it with your crush or partner. The interactive confession experience makes it more memorable than a text message.

### How does the birthday surprise work?
Visit [birthday.myheartcraft.com](https://birthday.myheartcraft.com) to customize a 3D birthday cake (flavors, candles, decorations), write a personal birthday letter, and select celebration animations. You get a unique link to share. The birthday person opens it to a full-screen animated celebration — candle blowing, confetti, and a personalized message reveal at the end.

### What makes the photo puzzle different from a regular puzzle?
The Surprise Photo Puzzle combines a jigsaw-style photo reveal with a Q&A challenge. You upload a photo and set custom questions. As the recipient answers questions and solves puzzle pieces, you can track their progress in real time on a separate live dashboard. It's a two-way experience, not just a solo activity.

### Does this work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. All three experiences are browser-based. They work on any modern phone (iPhone, Android), tablet, or desktop — no app download needed. Just open the link and the experience loads instantly.

### Can I use this for long-distance relationships?
Absolutely. Long-distance couples are one of the primary audiences. When you can't be physically present for a birthday, anniversary, or Valentine's Day, these shareable links let you create a personal moment. The Surprise Photo Puzzle's live dashboard lets you watch your partner solve it during a video call.

### Is this a game or a gift?
It's a **digital gift**. While the products have interactive elements (the dodging "No" button, the puzzle solving), the core purpose is to make someone feel special — not to score points or compete. Think of it as a personalized experience delivered via a link.

### What data does MyHeartCraft collect?
Each generated link contains private user content — names, messages, love letters, photos, and Q&A answers. These personalized pages are private and accessible only via the unique link shared by the sender. No email addresses, phone numbers, or social media logins are required from recipients.
